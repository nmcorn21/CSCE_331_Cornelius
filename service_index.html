Philanthropy

Currently I have the privilege of serving as the treasurer of a men’s organization on campus that has a very deserving philanthropy. The organization I am a part of is called Paradigm and we chose the philanthropy LLS, better known as the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. This cause is very near and dear to our organizations heart because of the way we originally started to support the cause. One of the founding members of our organization was diagnosed with non hodgkin's lymphoma during his time in college. The first philanthropy event our organization ever did was a movie night under the stars to help pay for his treatment. Thankfully since his diagnosis he has been in remission and made a full recovery. Since then we have stuck with LLS as our philanthropy to support this cause that is so near to our hearts. Our first few years we put on Movie Night Under the Stars to raise money for LLS, and in the last few years we have put on Rock ‘N’ Reel. At this event we have a few bands come out to perform and then show a movie after the sun goes down. I am very pleased to tell you about this event as it has a huge impact. Many of our members have family members affected by leukemia and lymphoma and it is still something we hold near to our hearts. In the past few years we have raised over $30,000 to donate and we are working on raising much more. To all of those reading this I hope that you would consider coming out to our philanthropy event next spring to support our cause. Tickets are never that much to buy and they come with fun, free stuff, and access to vendors. If you are interested at all follow Rock 'N' Reel and instagram to keep up with updates.
