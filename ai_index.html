Team History

Texas A&M Aggies football has a rich history dating back to 1894. As a powerhouse in college football, the Aggies have made significant contributions to the sport, with numerous conference championships and bowl game victories. The team has cultivated a passionate fan base known as the 12th Man, who play a vital role in the team's home-field advantage at Kyle Field.
